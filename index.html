Driving Academy Policies

  • All students must carry their valid BC driver's license during all in-car lessons an while sitting on drivers seat of the vehicle.
  • Each in-car lesson is a minimum of 60 minutes.
    • If the instructor is late, the lesson will commence at the time the student meets the instructor; the full lesson will be provided or the missing time will be added to a future lesson.
    • If the student is late, the lesson will commence at the time the lesson was scheduled; no extra time will be added.
  • One on one lesson. The lesson fee is $60 per 1 hour, $60 rental vehicle for road test. $170 for 1 hour warm up and use of vehicle for road test.
  • All lessons, including packages are not transferable to any party and at any circumstances.
  • The lesson fees must be paid in full prior to the commencement of the lesson.
  • The student is expected to complete lessons within 12 months from the date of payment; no refund or lessons will be given for incomplete sessions after 12 months
  • If you wish to cancel a driving lesson, we require 24 hours notice otherwise a cancellation fee of $30 for reschedule. No shows on scheduled lessons will be a forfit of the lesson.
  • In order to comply with safety of everyone, only the student and instructor will be allowed in the vehicle. One on one lesson, no guest or family member.
  • Refund Policy: All lessons / packages purchased are final sale and non-transferable.
  • The school don't accept any installment or upgrade package.
  • Driving test results will vary with each student; the school cannot guarantee all students will pass their driving test and obtain their driver's license
